<p>2021 was a difficult year for <a href="https://typesense.org/">Typesense</a>, but not in the way you would imagine.</p>
<p>The problem was not growth. We were growing comfortably month over month with little fuss.</p>
<p>But our competitors were raising millions of dollars and doubling their team sizes and their on-paper valuations.</p>
<p>Every other week a new angel investor or venture fund would reach out. Some wanted to genuinely learn about us, some wanted
to know more about the search space, and a few others were just curious about us because they were thinking of investing
in one of our rivals (it’s a small world, so word gets around!).</p>
