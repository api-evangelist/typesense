<p>When I first started working on <a href="https://github.com/typesense/typesense">Typesense</a> six years ago, I set myself a simple rule:</p>
<blockquote>
<p><em>I shall write some code everyday before or after work.</em></p></blockquote>
<p>That&rsquo;s it. No deadlines, no quarterly goals, no milestones.</p>
<p>I did not have a choice really — I was about to get married and was already working full-time in a demanding role.
As you can imagine, building a search engine from scratch is not a trivial undertaking, so that was my way of not having
to deal with additional stress.</p>
